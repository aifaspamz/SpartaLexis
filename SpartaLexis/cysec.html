What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity protects your digital life from threats like phishing and deepfakes. Phishing tricks you into revealing sensitive info, while deepfakes use AI to create realistic but false media. Stay vigilant with strong habits and tools to safeguard your data and identity.

Key Cybersecurity Threats: Focus on Deepfakes & Phishing

Phishing Attacks

Fraudulent attempts to steal information via deceptive emails, texts, or sites. Look for red flags like urgency, strange URLs, or unknown senders.

Deepfakes

AI-generated fake media designed to mislead or impersonate. Always verify sources and use detection tools for safety.

Related Risks: Malware & MITM

Malware often spreads through phishing links, while MITM attacks intercept your communication. Awareness helps minimize these risks.

Anti-Phishing & Anti-Deepfake Tips

  • Verify sender emails and inspect links before clicking.
  • Use reverse image search for suspicious media.
  • Enable 2FA and avoid oversharing personal info.
  • Install antivirus and keep your software updated.
  • Watch for unnatural facial movements or glitches in videos.
  • Report phishing attempts to authorities.

Additional Tips for Cybersecurity

Phishing Detection Tools

Use browser extensions like uBlock Origin or services like VirusTotal to scan suspicious links and emails.

Deepfake Detectors

Tools like Microsoft Video Authenticator or Hive Moderation help analyze media authenticity.

General Security

Use password managers such as LastPass and VPNs to protect your privacy.
